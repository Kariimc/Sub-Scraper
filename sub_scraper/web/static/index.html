Spotify

How do I get these keys?

Free, one-time setup — about 2 minutes:

  1. Open the Spotify Developer Dashboard and log in with your normal Spotify account.
  2. Click Create app. Give it any name (e.g. "Sub-Scraper") and description.
  3. In the Redirect URIs box, paste this exactly, then click Add: http://127.0.0.1:8888/callback Use 127.0.0.1 (not "localhost"), and keep the http:// and /callback. This is the #1 thing people get wrong.
  4. Tick Web API, accept the terms, and click Save.
  5. Open the app's Settings (top-right), then copy the Client ID and Client Secret into the boxes above.

⚠️ Spotify's login runs on your own computer, so load your Spotify library from the desktop app or the local web version (web_run.bat → localhost:8080). The hosted link works for SoundCloud, but can't finish the Spotify login.

SoundCloud

The part after soundcloud.com/ in your profile link — e.g. john-doe. This alone is enough for your public liked songs.
How do I get this token?

Only needed for playlists or private likes — leave it blank otherwise. Takes about a minute:

  1. Open soundcloud.com and make sure you're logged in.
  2. Press F12. A panel opens at the side or bottom — that's normal.
  3. Click the Application tab at the top of that panel. Can't see it? Click the » at the end of the tabs and pick it. On Firefox this tab is called Storage.
  4. On the left: open Cookies, then click https://soundcloud.com under it.
  5. Find the row named oauth_token.
  6. Double-click its value, copy it, and paste it in the box above. It looks like 2-123456-7890123-AbCdEf…

The token can expire (e.g. if you log out of SoundCloud). If playlists stop loading later, just grab a fresh one the same way.
